# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) - Gruntworks

### 1. Firmographics
- **Industry:** Residential HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning).
- **Company Size:** 3-10 technicians.
- **Revenue:** $500,000 - $2,000,000 annual revenue.
- **Location:** United States (Focus on Sunbelt states for high AC demand).

### 2. Psychographics
- **Pain Point:** Tired of "junk leads" from HomeAdvisor/Angi.
- **Pain Point:** Too busy to follow up with leads immediately.
- **Desire:** Predictable growth and higher-margin replacement jobs.
- **Tech Savviness:** Low to Medium (Needs a "done-for-you" solution).

### 3. The "Sweet Spot"
Owner-operators who are looking to step out of the truck and into a full-time management role. They have the capacity for more work but lack the marketing systems to fill the schedule.
