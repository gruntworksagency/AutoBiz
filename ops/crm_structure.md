# CRM Structure (V1 - Simple)

To be maintained in a Master Spreadsheet or Airtable until we hit $5k MRR.

### Columns:
1. **Company Name**
2. **Owner/Decision Maker Name**
3. **Email Address** (Verified)
4. **Phone Number**
5. **Website**
6. **Location** (City, State)
7. **Status** (Lead, Contacted, Follow-up, Meeting Booked, Closed, Lost)
8. **Last Contact Date**
9. **Notes** (e.g., Number of techs, current ads seen)

### Status Definitions:
- **Lead:** Scraped but not contacted.
- **Contacted:** Cold email/call sent.
- **Follow-up:** Responded but no meeting yet.
- **Meeting Booked:** Discovery call scheduled.
- **Closed:** Signed contract + Deposit paid.
