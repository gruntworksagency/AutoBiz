# Lead Generation Plan (First 100)

**Target:** HVAC Owners in Sunbelt States (TX, FL, AZ, GA).
**Criteria:** 3-10 Technicians, active website, Google Reviews > 20.

### Tools:
- **Primary:** Google Maps / D7 Lead Finder (for speed).
- **Verification:** MillionVerifier / NeverBounce (to protect domain health).

### Workflow:
1. **Scrape:** Extract 150 names (expecting 30% bounce/junk rate).
2. **Clean:** Run through verification.
3. **Enrich:** Find the owner's LinkedIn or personal email if the info@ address is the only one available.
4. **Upload:** Move to CRM (ops/crm_structure.md).

**Deadline:** 24 Hours.
